How to buy BitSharesPTS (Proto Shares) with USD ($)

See also: How to buy BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) with USD ($)

 

Step 1: Open a coinbase.com account and buy Bitcoin (BTC) with USD ($)

Step 2: Open a bter.com account and deposit Bitcoin (BTC)

Step 3: Buy BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) with Bitcoin (BTC) on bter.com

Step 4: Create a BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) address in your Protoshares-QT desktop client

Step 5: Withdraw BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) from bter.com and deposit in your Protoshares-QT desktop client

 

 

 

Step 1: Open a coinbase.com account and buy Bitcoin (BTC) with USD ($)

Goto https://coinbase.com. Enter your e-mail address, choose a password and click Create Bitcoin Wallet.

 

 

The following message will be displayed

 

 

Check your email and find the message from Coinbase

 

 

Click on the link in the email

 

 

You will be redirected to the Coinbase User Agreement. Review then click on Accept and Continue.

 

 

Follow the Coinbase Quickstart Guide to connect your Coinbase account to your bank account, then buy bitcoin equal to the amount of USD ($) you want to exchange for BitSharesPTS (Proto shares). This process can take anywhere from several days to two weeks due to transaction processing times.

For more information on buying Bitcoin (BTC) with USD ($), see Coinbase.com's How to Buy Bitcoin

 

 

 

 

Step 2: Open a bter.com account and deposit Bitcoin (BTC)

Goto http://bter.com/login

 

 

Fill in the fields under Sign Up and click Submit to create an account

 

 

Enter your account information under Login and click Submit

 

 

Locate the User Panel on the right side of the screen and click on BTC Deposit

 

 

Highlight the Bitcoin (BTC) Deposit address and select Edit -> Copy from the menu bar

 

 

Open a new browser window and go to https://coinbase.com/signin. Enter your Email and Password and click Sign In.

 

 

 

Select the Send Money button

 

 

Click on the To: field, then select Edit -> Paste from the menu to input the Bitcoin (BTC) deposit address from Bter and click Send Money

 

 

 

Step 3: Buy BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) with Bitcoin (BTC) on bter.com

Wait for your bitcoin depost to complete (credited after 1 confirmation, may take up to 10 minutes), then select PTS/BTC from the site menu bar of Bter.com

 

 

Locate the Buy PTS box.

 

 

Specify the price (in BTC) you would like to pay for each BitShare PTS (Proto share) (automatically filled with the lowest price currently available).

 

 

Then specify how many PTS you would like to buy and click Buy (BTC -> PTS).

 

 

 

Step 4: Create a BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) address in your Protoshares-QT desktop client

This step assumes that you have already downloaded and installed the Protoshares-QT desktop client. At this point in the guide, you have purchased your BitSharesPTS (Proto shares), however, the private keys are stored on Bter. IT IS HIGHLY RECOMMENDED that you follow the steps below to withdraw the BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) from Bter and send them to an address generated by your Protoshares-QT desktop client so you control the private keys.

 

Open your Protoshares-QT desktop client and click Receive

 

 

Click on New Address

 

 

Create a label for your new address and click OK

 

 

Select your new address

 

 

Click on copy Address

 

 

Step 5: Withdraw BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) from bter.com and deposit in your Protoshares-QT desktop client

Go to your Account page on bter.com by clicking on your Account Name in the User Panel

 

 

Click on Other crypto-currencies deposit and withdrawal

 

 

Scroll down until you see ProtoShare PTS and select Withdraw

 

 

Enter your Fund password, amount of PTS to withdraw and the verification code. Then, click on the PTS Address field and select Edit -> Paste from the menu bar to paste the PTS address that you generated in the Protoshares-QT desktop client. Click on Submit request.

 

 

Congratulations!

You have purchased BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) with your USD ($)! Be patient, withdrawals from Bter can take up to several hours to appear in the balance of your Protoshares-QT desktop client.

 

Notes about BitSharesPTS (Proto shares):

- At least 10% of future BitShares will be allocated to holders of BitSharesPTS

- BitSharesPTS is a Bitcoin clone that allows you to buy and trade ownership in future BitShares created by Invictus Innovations or derived from their software.
