Option 1 - Buy BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) with Bitcoin (BTC):

Step 1: Go to How to buy BitSharesPTS (Proto shares) with USD ($) and complete Step 1 to buy Bitcoin (BTC) with USD ($)

 

Step 2: Create a Blockchain.info wallet

Go to https://blockchain.info/wallet and click on Create My Free Wallet

 

 

Fill in the fields under Create A New Wallet and click Continue

 

 

Write down your Wallet Recovery Mnemonic and click Continue

 

 

Step 3: Transfer Bitcoin (BTC) from Coinbase to your Blockchain.info wallet

Type in your password and click Open Wallet

 

 

Click on Receive Money

 

 

Click on New Address

 

 

Enter a Label for your new address and click Save

 

 

Highlight the new address and select Edit -> Copy from the menu bar

 

 

Open a new browser window and go to https://coinbase.com/signin. Enter your Email and Password and click Sign In.

 

 

Select the Send Money button

 

 

Click on the To: field, then select Edit -> Paste from the menu to input the Bitcoin (BTC) address from your Blockchain.info wallet. Enter the amount of bitcoin you would like to transfer to Blockchain.info to buy BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) then click Send Money.

 

 

Step 4: Buy BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) with Bitcoin (BTC)

Log in to your wallet on Blockchain.info and click on Send Money

 

 

Enter the BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) Donation address 1ANGELwQwWxMmbdaSWhWLqBEtPTkWb8uDc in the To: field, enter the amount of Bitcoin (BTC) you would like to convert to BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) and click Send Payment

 

 

Congratulations!

You have purchased BitSharesAGS (Angel shares) with your USD ($)!
